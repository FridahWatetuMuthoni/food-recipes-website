About Savory Kitchen

Authentic Flavors for Every Home

At Savory Kitchen, our recipes are born from a passion for bold, international flavors and high-quality ingredients. Each guide is meticulously tested to ensure that you can recreate restaurant-quality meals in your own home. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned home chef, our step-by-step instructions are designed to build your culinary confidence.

Crafted with Fire and Spice

Looking for something specific? Our library ranges from healthy Mediterranean bowls to the signature flame-grilled Portuguese chicken that defined our start. If you don't see a recipe you need, our Culinary Request service allows you to tell us what you're craving, and our chefs will get to work developing a foolproof guide just for you.

The Secret to the Perfect Char.

Authentic Peri-Peri isn't just a sauce; it's a centuries-old tradition of fire and flavor. We combine hand-picked bird's eye chilies with fresh lemon zest and cold-pressed olive oil to create a marinade that doesn't just sit on the meat—it transforms it.

By utilizing a high-heat sear followed by a slow-roast, we ensure every recipe results in that signature smoky crust and succulent interior.
