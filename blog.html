    The Art of the 24-Hour Marinade

    Bring the Heat.

    The secret to that signature deep-red hue and falling-off-the-bone tenderness in Portuguese chicken isn't just the grill—it's the patience. In this week's guide, we explore the chemistry of acidity and spice. By balancing fresh lemon juice with cold-pressed olive oil and our triple-filtered Bird's Eye chilies, you create a marinade that tenderizes the protein while infusing it with layers of citrusy heat. We discuss why a minimum of 24 hours is required to let the garlic and smoked paprika truly penetrate the meat, ensuring every bite is as flavorful as the last.

The Secret to the Perfect Char.

Authentic Peri-Peri isn't just a sauce; it's a centuries-old tradition of fire and flavor. We combine hand-picked bird's eye chilies with fresh lemon zest and cold-pressed olive oil to create a marinade that doesn't just sit on the meat—it transforms it.

By utilizing a high-heat sear followed by a slow-roast, we ensure every recipe results in that signature smoky crust and succulent interior.
